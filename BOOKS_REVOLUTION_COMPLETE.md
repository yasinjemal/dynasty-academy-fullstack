## 🎓 How to Use

### Quick Start (30 seconds)

```bash
1. Navigate to http://localhost:3000/admin/books
2. Click "Add New Book"
3. Enter book title
4. Click "✨ Generate with AI"
5. Wait 3-5 seconds
6. Review results
7. Click "✅ Apply to Form"
```
